Video Analysis

April 4, 2024 Brandon Wenaas

Above is a screenshot of the editing screen in Dartfish, a sports video analysis program. As an example, I have recorded myself passing a volleyball, annotating the video with pointers and critiques on my form. Video analysis of practice and competition can be a powerful tool for helping athletes improve, as it provides visual evidence that validates feedback given by a coach.

In particular, I tried to draw attention to my upright passing position and poor feet alignment, as these are two highly impactful, yet easy to fix, flaws in passing form. The annotated video can be viewed below.
